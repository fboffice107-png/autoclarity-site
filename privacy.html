Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

AutoClarity is an informational vehicle-guidance app designed to help users better understand possible vehicle issues based on symptoms and guided diagnostic logic. AutoClarity does not provide guaranteed diagnoses, repair instructions, safety certifications, or professional mechanical advice.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect or receive limited information needed to operate the app and provide purchased features, such as symptom selections, report-related inputs, app usage information, purchase status, subscription status, entitlement status, and basic technical information.

2. Purchases and Subscriptions

AutoClarity may offer one-time report unlocks and auto-renewing subscriptions. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

We use RevenueCat to help manage in-app purchase and subscription status, entitlements, and related purchase validation.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide app functionality and access to purchased content
  • Verify purchases, subscriptions, and entitlements
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and performance
  • Improve the app and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How Information May Be Shared

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the app, including subscription and purchase infrastructure providers, hosting providers, analytics providers, or technical service providers, but only as reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the service.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to enforce our rights, or to protect users, vehicles, or the public from harm.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, enforce our agreements, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

AutoClarity is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Informational Use Only

AutoClarity provides informational estimates and guidance only. Results depend on user input and app logic and may be incomplete, inaccurate, or not suitable for a specific vehicle, condition, or situation.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of AutoClarity after any update means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at: support@getautoclarity.com
