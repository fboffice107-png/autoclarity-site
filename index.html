Your report

Every AutoClarity report includes

Report-style results you can save, print, or show a repair shop.

  • The most likely cause

    A clear, plain-English explanation of what the problem most likely is — and why.

  • Safety-first guidance

    Driving-safety guidance on every result — always visible, never paywalled.

  • Repair-cost direction

    An estimated repair-cost range, so a quote doesn’t catch you off guard.

  • Severity level

    How serious the issue looks and whether it may be urgent — at a glance.

  • Recommended next steps

    What to consider doing next, in order — including what to ask the shop.

  • A shareable PDF report

    Save it, print it, or show it to the repair shop so everyone starts on the same page.

AutoClarity can help when your vehicle…

  • Won’t start
  • Runs rough or loses power
  • Overheats
  • Makes unusual noises
  • Has braking concerns
  • Shows warning lights
  • Has electrical, steering, or suspension symptoms
  • Has heating or A/C symptoms
